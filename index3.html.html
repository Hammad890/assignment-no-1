About us

Kids Mall aspires to provide everything for children between 0-14 years of age, under one roof. Since its inception,Kids Mall aims to revolutionize the phenomenon of retail shopping for parents and kids across Pakistan. We look forward to delight our customers with extraordinary shopping experience; both in-stores and online.We had launched our first store in Karachi in 2016, Kids Mall promises to surprise and delight the guests with unique and high-quality merchandise. The products offered include garments, toys and all the way to infant products. Currently we have 10 stores all over pakistan
